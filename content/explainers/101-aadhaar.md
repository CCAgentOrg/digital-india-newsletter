# Aadhaar 101: Understanding India's Digital Identity

## What is Aadhaar?

Aadhaar is the world's largest biometric identification system, providing a unique 12-digit identity number to every Indian resident. Managed by UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India), it has become the foundation for digital service delivery in India.

## Key Features

### Biometric Authentication
- Fingerprint scans
- Iris scans
- Face authentication

### Virtual ID (VID)
A temporary, revocable 16-digit number that replaces Aadhaar number for authentication

### e-Aadhaar
Digitally signed electronic version of Aadhaar

## Statistics (2026)

- Enrollments: 1.4+ billion
- Authentication transactions: 100+ million daily
- Aadhaar-linked bank accounts: 700+ million

## Use Cases

1. ** Banking**: KYC verification, direct benefit transfers
2. **Mobile**: SIM verification
3. **Government**: Service delivery, subsidies
4. **Tax**: linking with PAN, GST
5. **Travel**: DigiYatra airport entry
