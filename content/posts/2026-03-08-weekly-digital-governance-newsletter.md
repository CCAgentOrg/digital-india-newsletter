## 2. Aadhaar/UIDAI Updates

### Offline Aadhaar Verification Expands

UIDAI has strengthened Aadhaar's offline verification capabilities:
- **Offline eKYC XML**: Aadhaar holders can now generate XML files for offline verification without biometrics, allowing businesses to verify identity without real-time connectivity
- **m-Aadhaar Integration**: The mobile app now supports offline QR code generation for verification purposes
- **Paperless eKYC**: API-based verification continues to expand across banking, telecom, and government services

### Aadhaar Update Process 2026

UIDAI has announced that the [free online Aadhaar update facility has been extended until June 14, 2026](https://www.thehindu.com/sci-tech/technology/government-to-upskill-reskill-it-workforce-amid-ai-challenges-ashwini-vaishnaw/article70650559.ece). Users can update their address and upload supporting documents without fees through the myAadhaar portal.

### PhonePe IPO Preparation

Walmart-backed PhonePe is finalizing plans for its much-anticipated IPO:
- **Valuation Target**: $9-10.5 billion
- **Expected Timing**: April 2026, subject to market conditions
- **Key Stakeholders**: Walmart trimming ~12% stake; Tiger Global and Microsoft looking to exit

This would be one of the largest Indian fintech IPOs and marks a significant milestone in the country's digital payments ecosystem. ## 4. Policy & Regulatory Updates

### Deepfake Regulation via IT Rules Amendment

The government has amended the IT Rules to address deepfake concerns:
- **Intermediary Accountability**: Social media platforms must detect and remove AI-generated deceptive content
- **User Reporting Mechanism**: Platforms required to implement complaint mechanisms for synthetic media
- **Penalties**: Non-compliance can result in loss of intermediary protections under Section 79

This amendment positions India among the early adopters of specific AI-generated content regulations. [Read more on Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/tech/technology/govt-amends-it-rules-to-curb-deepfakes-improve-grievance-redressal/articleshow/129044418.cms)

### Budget 2026: Data Center Tax Incentives

The Union Budget 2026-27 introduces significant incentives for data center investments:
- **Tax Benefits**: Special tax deductions for companies setting up data centers in India
- **Timeline**: Incentives apply to investments made in FY 2026-27 through FY 2029-30
- **Target**: Encourage $50+ billion in data center investments by 2030
- **Strategic Goal**: Position India as a global data center hub to meet growing AI and cloud computing demand

TCS CEO K Krithivasan noted India will need approximately 10 GW of AI data center capacity by 2030. [Read more on The Hindu](https://www.thehindu.com/business/budget-paved-way-for-tech-investment-with-tax-certainty/article70648766.ece)
